English · 中文

Effective date: 2025‑01‑01 · Package: com.eggthrow

Stress Relief: Egg Throw (“the App”) is a casual game. We do not require account creation and we do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) from you within the App.

Data we process

  • Advertising data — The App uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), approximate location, and ad interaction data to provide, measure, and improve advertising. See Google’s Advertising Policy.
  • Crash and diagnostics — Basic, non‑PII diagnostics may be generated by the operating system and Google Play for stability and fraud prevention.
  • User‑provided content — If you use the avatar upload feature, the selected image is processed locally on your device to create a pixelated target. The image is not uploaded to our servers.

Permissions

  • INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required for loading ads and game assets.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID — allows AdMob to access the device Advertising ID.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If your target audience includes children, AdMob can be configured for child‑directed treatment (e.g., maxAdContentRating, limited ad personalization).

Third‑party services

  • Google AdMob — AdMob Privacy
  • Google Play — Google Privacy Policy

Data retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Ad data is processed by Google according to their policies.

Your choices

  • Reset or limit the device Advertising ID in Android settings.
  • Opt‑out of personalized ads where available.
  • Revoke storage/media permissions (if requested in future versions).

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: mmluckyteam@gmail.com. Replace this with your real contact email.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above.
